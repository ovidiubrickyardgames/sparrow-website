# Pre-Delivery Checklist

Run every piece of copy through this before delivering:

## Structure
- [ ] Does the headline/first line work with zero surrounding context?
- [ ] Is there exactly one CTA, and is it unambiguous?
- [ ] Does the piece address one reader, one problem, one promise?
- [ ] Is the awareness-stage pitch correct (see SKILL.md Step 2)?

## Language
- [ ] Any sentence removable with zero loss of meaning? Cut it.
- [ ] Any generic superlative ("best," "amazing," "revolutionary") without a fact backing it up? Replace with the fact.
- [ ] Any invented stat, quote, or claim not given by the user? Remove or flag.
- [ ] Passive voice where active would be punchier? Fix it.
- [ ] Sentence length varied, or does everything plod at the same rhythm?

## Banned / red-flag words and phrases
Cut or replace on sight unless the user's voice explicitly calls for them:
"leverage," "synergy," "seamless/seamlessly," "unlock," "revolutionize/revolutionary," "game-changing," "elevate your," "in today's fast-paced world," "in today's [X] landscape," "solutions" (as a noun for a product), "cutting-edge," "state-of-the-art," "empower," "robust," "holistic," "best-in-class," "world-class," "delve," "unleash," "supercharge," "next-level," "at the end of the day," "it goes without saying," "needless to say."

## Final gut check
Read it out loud. If it sounds like an ad reading itself, rewrite. If it sounds like a smart, direct friend explaining why this is worth your time, ship it.
